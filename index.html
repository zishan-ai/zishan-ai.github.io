Summary

I am an AI researcher with experience across natural language processing, generative AI, automated code generation and agentic AI systems. My work spans controllable text generation (style, sentiment, knowledge, persona), dialogue agents, evaluation of complex multi‑agent systems, and multi‑modal retrieval‑augmented generation.

I enjoy working at the intersection of research and engineering—turning ideas into reliable systems that can be deployed in real‑world products. I publish in leading AI and NLP venues and collaborate with academia and industry on problems where language technologies can have direct social and business impact.
